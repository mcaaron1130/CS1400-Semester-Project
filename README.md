Hello!

This project is called the Media Store. You can select from a variety of books or movies.
At the start of the program, you can enter "1" or "2" to buy movies or books.
You can select up to 10 books and 10 movies.
You can add items to your cart by entering the corresponding number.
You can also remove items from your cart by entering "-1."
If you end up buying fewer than 10 books or movies, you can enter "0" to proceed to checkout.
If you're done shopping in the first category, you can continue shopping in another category.
For example, if you've finished buying books, you can buy movies too.
